Steps offers a variety of widgets so you can see your step count, streaks, and workout data without opening the app.

## Widget Types

| Widget | Sizes | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **Today Steps** | Small, Lock Screen (rectangular, inline, circular) | Current step count and goal progress |
| **Monthly Calendar** | Medium | Calendar grid showing daily goal completion for the month |
| **Yearly Calendar** | Medium, Large | Year-at-a-glance calendar with goal completion heatmap |
| **Hourly Chart** | Medium | Bar chart of steps by hour for today |
| **Weekly Chart** | Medium | Bar chart of steps for the past 7 days |
| **Monthly Chart** | Medium | Bar chart of steps for the current month |
| **Streak** | Small, Medium | Current streak count and weekly goal progress |
| **Workout Highlights** | Small | Your most recent workout stats |

## Live Activity

During an active workout, a Live Activity appears on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island (iOS 26+). It shows:

- Workout type icon
- Elapsed time
- Distance covered
- Calories burned
- Current pace

The Dynamic Island cycles between stats every 30 seconds.

## Adding Widgets

<Steps>
  <Step title="Long-press your Home Screen">
    Touch and hold an empty area on your Home Screen until the apps start jiggling.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the + button">
    Tap the **+** button in the top-left corner to open the widget gallery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for Steps">
    Type "Steps" in the search bar to find all available widgets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a widget">
    Select the widget type and size you want, then tap **Add Widget**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Lock Screen Widgets

The Today Steps widget is also available for your Lock Screen in three styles:

- **Rectangular** - Shows step count and progress bar
- **Inline** - Compact step count in the date line
- **Circular** - Step count in a round gauge

<Tip>Your widgets automatically update with your latest step data and reflect your chosen color theme.</Tip>
