## Overview

The Workout Flight Ticket is a boarding pass-styled card in the Insights screen that gives you a stylised summary of all your workouts for the year. It uses airline ticket visual language â€” a route, a perforated tear line, and a QR code â€” to make your yearly fitness feel like a journey worth celebrating.

It only appears in Insights when you have at least one workout recorded for the selected year.

## User Experience

The ticket sits at the top of the Workouts section in Insights, directly after the "Workouts" section header.

## How It Works

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Workouts" icon="dumbbell">
    The total number of workouts you completed during the year, displayed prominently along the flight path.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Duration" icon="clock">
    Your combined workout time for the year, shown as hours and minutes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Distance" icon="route">
    Total distance covered across all workouts, in km or miles based on your unit system.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Calories" icon="fire">
    Total active calories burned across all workouts, in kcal.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  The boarding message in the stub rotates based on your total workout count, so it will vary from year to year as your activity level changes.
</Tip>
