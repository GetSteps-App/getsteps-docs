The Settings screen lets you customize how Steps looks and works. Access it from the gear icon on the Home Dashboard.

## Profile

{/* TODO: Screenshot of profile card in Settings */}

Your profile card appears at the top of Settings. Tap it to edit your display name, avatar photo, and country. If you're not signed in, you'll see a prompt to **Sign In with Apple**.

Pro subscribers see a **Pro** badge on their profile card.

| Setting | Options | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Appearance** | System, Light, Dark | Follow your device or set manually |
| **Color Theme** | 7 presets + Custom | Pro feature. See [Color Themes](/features/color-themes) |
| **App Icon** | 17 icons | See [App Icon](/features/app-icon-selector) |
| **Home Insight** | Off, Message, Average | What shows below your step count |
| **Show Weather** | On, Off | Display temperature and conditions |

## General

| Setting | Options | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| **Unit System** | Metric, Imperial | Affects distance display (km or mi) |
| **Week Starts On** | Sunday, Monday | Changes calendar and weekly views |
| **View Steps By** | Day, Week, Month | Week and Month are Pro features |
| **Daily Goal** | 1,000 - 20,000 | See [Daily Goal](/features/daily-goal) |
| **Notifications** | On, Off | Pro feature. See [Notifications](/features/notifications) |
| **App Lock** | On, Off | Pro feature. See [App Lock](/features/app-lock) |
| **App Language** | 10 languages | Opens your device language settings |

### Notification Sub-Settings

Tapping Notifications opens a detail screen with individual toggles:

- **Goal Achievement** — Goal reached and milestones
- **Workout Completion** — When a workout finishes
- **App Unlock** — When apps are unlocked
- **Daily Reminders** — Morning, afternoon, and evening

### Supported Languages

Steps is available in English, Deutsch, Español, Français, Italiano, 日本語, 한국어, Português (Brasil), Русский, and 简体中文.
