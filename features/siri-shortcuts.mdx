Steps integrates with Siri so you can check your daily step count using voice commands without opening the app.

## Available Shortcut

### Today's Steps

Ask Siri for your current step count. Steps will respond with your total steps for today.

## How to Use

Simply say any of these phrases to Siri:

- "Get my steps from Steps"
- "How many steps today in Steps"
- "What's my step count in Steps"
- "Show my steps from Steps"
- "Check my steps in Steps"
- "How far have I walked in Steps"

<Tip>You can also add "Today's Steps" to the Shortcuts app to create custom automations or add it to your Home Screen.</Tip>
