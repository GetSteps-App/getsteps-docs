| Style | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Transparent** | Your workout stats on a transparent background, ready to overlay on any image |
| **Overlay** | Stats overlaid directly on your route map |
| **Frame** | Your route map with a clean bordered stats frame |
| **Photo** | Stats overlaid on a photo you choose from your library |

<Tip>Overlay and Frame styles are available for outdoor workouts with GPS routes.</Tip>

## Aspect Ratios

Select the best ratio for your sharing destination:

| Ratio | Best For |
|-------|----------|
| **16:9** | YouTube, landscape posts |
| **4:3** | Presentations, landscape photos |
| **1:1** | Instagram feed, profile pictures |
| **3:4** | Portrait photos |
| **9:16** | Instagram Stories, TikTok, Reels |

## Map Options

For Overlay and Frame styles, customize the map appearance:

- **Map Type** - Standard or Satellite
- **Appearance** - Light or Dark
- **Point of Interest** - Include All or Exclude All (Standard map only)
- **Elevation Style** - Flat or Realistic (Satellite map only)

## Save to Photos

<Note>Saving to Photos is a Pro feature.</Note>

Tap the save button to export your styled workout image as a PNG directly to your photo library.

## GPX Export

<Note>GPX export is a Pro feature.</Note>

Export your workout route as a `.gpx` file for use in other fitness apps, mapping tools, or route planners. The GPX file includes your complete GPS track with timestamps.
