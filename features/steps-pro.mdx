## Subscription Plans

Steps Pro is available as individual or family plans:

| Plan | Billing | Trial |
|------|---------|-------|
| **Monthly** | Billed monthly | 7-day free trial |
| **Yearly** | Billed annually (best value) | None |
| **Lifetime** | One-time purchase | None |

<Tip>Family plans let you share your Steps Pro subscription with up to 5 family members through Apple's Family Sharing.</Tip>

## Free Trial

The Monthly plan includes a **7-day free trial**. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel anytime during the trial period.

## Restore Purchase

If you've previously purchased Steps Pro:

1. Open the Steps Pro screen
2. Tap **Restore Purchase** at the bottom
3. Your subscription will be restored automatically

## Redeem a Code

If you have a promo or offer code:

1. Open the Steps Pro screen
2. Tap **Redeem** to enter your code
3. The subscription will be applied to your account

## Manage Your Subscription

To change or cancel your subscription, go to **Settings > Manage Subscription** in the Steps app. This opens your Apple subscription management page.
