The Activity List shows all your recorded workouts in one place, organized by month for easy browsing.

## Viewing Your Workouts

Your workouts are grouped by month, with the most recent at the top. Each entry shows:

- Workout type and icon
- Duration
- Date and time
- Distance (for outdoor workouts)

## Filtering by Type

Use the filter options to show only specific workout types:

- Walking
- Running
- Cycling
- Hiking
- All other HealthKit workout types (swimming, yoga, HIIT, etc.)

This helps you quickly find a specific workout when you have a long history.

## History Limits

| Plan | History Access |
|------|---------------|
| **Free** | Last 3 months |
| **Pro** | Unlimited history |

<Note>Upgrade to Steps Pro to access your complete workout history beyond the last 3 months.</Note>

## Workout Details

Tap any workout in the list to open its [Activity Detail](/docs/features/activity-detail) view with full stats, route map, and sharing options.
