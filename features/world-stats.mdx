## What You'll See

- **Total members** — How many people are part of the Steps community.
- **Total steps** — All steps logged by the community combined.
- **Total distance** — The combined distance walked, shown in your preferred units.
- **Your position** — Your global rank (for example, **#5**) and your percentile (for example, **Top 5%**). If you haven't logged steps yet, you'll see **Not ranked yet**.
- **Average steps per active user** — The average across everyone who has logged at least one step.
- **Top countries** — The leading countries by total steps, each with its flag and step count.

<Tip>Log some steps to appear on the leaderboard and earn your global rank.</Tip>
