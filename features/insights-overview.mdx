Insights gives you a year-in-review of your step and workout data with stats, records, and fun comparisons.

## Steps Analytics

Your yearly step summary includes:

- **Yearly Receipt** - Total steps, distance, and calories for the year
- **Daily Average** - Your average steps per day
- **Best Day** - Your highest single-day step count and the date
- **Best Month** - The month with the most total steps
- **Peak Hour** - The hour of day when you're most active
- **Weekday Breakdown** - Chart comparing steps by day of the week, with weekday vs weekend averages
- **Goals Met** - Total number of days you reached your step goal
- **Best Streak** - Your longest consecutive streak in days

## Workout Analytics

If you've recorded workouts, you'll also see:

- **Workout Flight Ticket** - Total workouts, workout time, distance, and calories
- **Workout Breakdown** - Most common workout type, outdoor vs indoor counts, weekday vs weekend activity

## Personal Records

{/* TODO: Screenshot of personal records grid */}
<Frame caption="Personal records grid showing all-time bests">
  <img src="/images/screenshots/insights-personal-records.jpg" alt="Personal records" />
</Frame>

A scrollable grid shows your 8 all-time personal records:

| Record | What It Tracks |
|--------|----------------|
| **Fastest** | Best pace |
| **Most Burned** | Most calories in a single workout |
| **Earliest** | Earliest workout start time |
| **Most Climb** | Greatest elevation gain |
| **Longest** | Longest workout duration |
| **Farthest** | Greatest workout distance |
| **Latest** | Latest workout start time |
| **Max HR** | Highest heart rate recorded |

Tap any record to see the full workout detail.

## Year Picker

Use the year picker in the toolbar to switch between years and compare your activity over time.
