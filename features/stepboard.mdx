The Stepboard is a daily leaderboard where you compete with walkers around the world — or in private groups with people you invite. See how your steps stack up, climb the rankings, and stay motivated. Join or leave anytime.

## How to Join

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In with Apple">
    Tap the trophy icon on the Home Dashboard. If you're not signed in, you'll be prompted to sign in with your Apple ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join Stepboard">
    Tap **Join Stepboard** to enter the daily leaderboard. Your steps sync automatically from Apple Health.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Profile">
    Add a display name, avatar photo, and country. Your country appears as a flag emoji next to your name. All fields are optional — you'll show as "Anonymous" if you skip the name.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The leaderboard ranks the top 100 walkers each day by step count. Each entry shows:

- **Rank** — Top 3 get medal icons (🥇🥈🥉), others show a number
- **Avatar** — Your profile photo or initials placeholder
- **Name & Country** — Display name with country flag emoji
- **Steps** — Daily step count with thousands separators

Your own entry is highlighted so you can quickly spot your position.

## Your Profile

Tap your profile to edit your display name, avatar photo, and country at any time. Changes appear immediately on the leaderboard.

- **Display Name** — Up to 50 characters. Shows "Anonymous" if not set
- **Avatar** — Take a photo, choose from your library, or remove it
- **Country** — Shown as a flag emoji next to your name

## Date Navigation

Browse leaderboards from past days using the date bar at the bottom of the screen. Tap the left or right arrows to move between days, or tap the date label to jump to a specific date with the date picker.

<Tip>You can view any past day's leaderboard, but you can't see future dates.</Tip>

## Syncing

Your steps sync automatically from Apple Health. Data for each day syncs overnight once the day is complete, so you'll see yesterday's final rankings the next morning. Pull down on the leaderboard to refresh and get the latest data.

## Leaving the Stepboard

To leave, tap the menu button (⋯) in the top-right corner and select **Leave Stepboard**. Your data is removed from the leaderboard. You can rejoin anytime, but your previous history won't be restored.

## Privacy

The Stepboard is fully opt-in. Only these details are visible to other users:

- Display name (or "Anonymous")
- Avatar photo (if set)
- Country flag (if set)
- Daily step count

<Tip>If you prefer not to share personal details, simply skip setting a name and avatar. You'll appear as "Anonymous" with a generic placeholder.</Tip>

No other health data, location data, or personal information is shared.

## Groups

Groups turn the Stepboard into a private leaderboard for you and your friends, family, or coworkers. Each group has its own daily ranking alongside the World board — switch between them from the board selector at the top of the Stepboard.

### Create a Group

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Stepboard menu">
    Tap the menu button (⋯) in the top-right corner and choose **Create group**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your group">
    Enter a name and tap **Create**. You'll be asked to sign in with Apple if you haven't already.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share the invite">
    A unique 4-character invite code is generated. Copy the code or share the link with friends.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You don't need to join the World leaderboard to create or use groups.

### Join a Group

There are two ways to join:

- **Invite link** — Tap a `getsteps.app/join/<code>` link shared by a friend. The app opens a preview showing the group name, member count, and who invited you. Tap **Join** to enter.
- **Invite code** — Open the Stepboard menu and tap **Join group**, then enter the 4-character code.

### Group Leaderboard

Each group has its own daily leaderboard ranking members by steps or distance. Switch boards from the title menu at the top of the Stepboard — the list shows **World** plus every group you belong to.

### Manage Members

While viewing a group, tap the menu (⋯) and choose **Manage group** to:

- **Rename the group** — admin only
- **Copy or share the invite code and link**
- **View all members** with avatars, display names, and country flags
- **Remove members** — admin only

### Sharing Invites

Use **Share group** from the Stepboard menu, or the share button in Manage group, to send a `getsteps.app/join/<code>` link through Messages, Mail, or any share sheet target. Recipients who already have Steps installed jump straight to the join preview.

### Leaving or Deleting a Group

Open **Manage group** and tap:

- **Leave group** — you can rejoin later with the same invite code
- **Delete group** — admin only; removes all members and ends the group

<Tip>Group invite codes are private. Anyone with the code can join, so only share with people you trust.</Tip>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need an account to use the Stepboard?">
    Yes, you need to sign in with Apple to join the Stepboard or any group. This is used to identify your entries on the leaderboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I invite friends to a group?">
    Open the group in the Stepboard and tap the menu (⋯) → **Share group**, or open **Manage group** and tap **Share link**. Anyone who opens the link on an iPhone with Steps installed sees a preview and can join with one tap.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I be in multiple groups?">
    Yes. Switch between groups — and the World board — from the title menu at the top of the Stepboard.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How many people appear on the leaderboard?">
    The leaderboard shows the top 100 walkers for each day, ranked by step count.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can others see my personal information?">
    Only your display name, avatar, country flag, and step count are visible. No other health or personal data is shared.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I leave and rejoin?">
    You start fresh. Your previous leaderboard history is removed and won't be restored when you rejoin.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why don't I see today's complete data?">
    Today's steps are still being counted, so the leaderboard syncs each day's final data overnight. Check back tomorrow for the complete rankings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
