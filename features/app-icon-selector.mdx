## Changing Your Icon

1. Open **Settings**
2. Tap **App icon**
3. Browse the icon grid and tap your choice
4. iOS will confirm the icon change

<Tip>Each icon has a dark variant that matches your system's dark mode. The icon automatically adapts to your device's appearance setting.</Tip>
