Color themes let you customize the look of Steps by changing the colors used for your progress ring, charts, and widgets.

<Note>Color themes are a Pro feature. Free users use the default Classic theme.</Note>

## Preset Themes

Steps includes 7 built-in color themes. Each theme has two colors: one for in-progress and one for goal-completed states.

| Theme | In Progress | Completed |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| **Classic** | Orange | Green |
| **Slate** | Slate Blue | Blue |
| **Coral** | Coral | Rose |
| **Forest** | Amber | Emerald |
| **Twilight** | Violet | Indigo |
| **Teal** | Teal | Cyan |
| **Blush** | Pink | Purple |

## Custom Colors

Choose your own colors with the custom theme option. You can set separate colors for:

- **In Progress** - The color shown while you're working toward your goal
- **Completed** - The color shown once you've reached your goal

Use the iOS color picker to select any color you like.

## Where Themes Apply

Your selected theme colors appear throughout the app:

- Progress ring on the Home Dashboard
- Chart bars and highlights
- Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets
- Streak badge accents

<Tip>Changing your theme automatically updates your widgets to match.</Tip>

## How to Change Your Theme

1. Open **Settings**
2. Tap **Color Theme**
3. Select a preset or tap **Custom** to pick your own colors
