title: "Home Dashboard"
description: "Your daily step tracking command center with charts, progress, and weather"
---

The Home Dashboard is your main view in Steps, showing your step count, activity metrics, progress toward your daily goal, and step charts.

## Step Count Card

The main card displays your current activity:

- **Step Count** - Your total steps for the selected period
- **Distance** - Walking and running distance in km or miles
- **Calories** - Active energy burned
- **Flights Climbed** - Floors climbed (daily view only)

A **progress ring** surrounds the card, filling up as you approach your daily goal. The ring color changes to your "completed" theme color once you hit your goal.

## Streak Badge

Your current streak count appears in the top-left corner with a flame icon. Tap it to view your full streak details, heatmap, and badge progress.

## Motivational Messages

You can choose what appears below your step count in **Settings > Home insight**:

- **Message** - A motivational quote or encouragement
- **Average** - How your steps compare to your daily average
- **Off** - Show nothing extra

## Charts

Step charts appear below the main card based on your selected view mode:

| View | Chart | Access |
|------|-------|--------|
| **Daily** | Hourly step bar chart | Free |
| **Weekly** | 7-day step bar chart | Pro |
| **Monthly** | Monthly step bar chart | Pro |

<Note>Weekly and Monthly views are Pro features. You can switch between views in **Settings > View steps by**.</Note>

In weekly and monthly views, you can toggle between **Total** and **Average** steps using the button in the top-right corner.

## Date Navigation

Swipe **left** or **right** on the step card to navigate between days, weeks, or months. A horizontal scrollable calendar strip at the bottom lets you jump to any date quickly.

## Weather

When enabled in Settings, the current temperature and humidity display in the top toolbar. Toggle this in **Settings > Show weather**.

## Refresh

Tap the refresh button in the top-right corner to manually sync your latest step data from Apple Health.
