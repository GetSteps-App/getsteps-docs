AI Coach reads your workout and gives you personalized feedback right in the workout detail view — a short assessment, numbered tips to improve, and a suggestion for next time.

<Note>AI Coach runs entirely on-device using Apple Intelligence. It requires an iPhone that supports Apple Intelligence on iOS 26 or later. The assessment is free for everyone; **Tips** and **Try next time** require Steps Pro.</Note>

Open any workout from your history and scroll down to the **AI Coach** section. Once your workout metrics finish loading, AI Coach automatically generates feedback based on your activity — including heart rate, pace, splits, distance, elevation, and duration.

The feedback is tailored to the type of workout, so the advice you get for a run differs from a ride or a strength session.

## What You Get

- **Assessment** — A short summary of how your workout went. Free for everyone.
- **Tips** — Numbered, actionable suggestions to help you improve. *(Steps Pro)*
- **Try next time** — One idea to focus on in your next session. *(Steps Pro)*

While the feedback is being prepared, you'll see an **Analyzing your workout…** message. You can refresh to regenerate the feedback at any time.

## Chat About Your Workout

After the feedback finishes, tap **Chat with your workout data** to ask follow-up questions. See [Workout Chat](/features/workout-chat) for details.

<Tip>AI Coach uses only the data from the workout you're viewing. The more complete your workout data (heart rate, route, splits), the more detailed your feedback.</Tip>
