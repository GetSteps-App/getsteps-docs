title: "Notifications"

---

Steps sends notifications to celebrate your progress and keep you motivated throughout the day.

<Note>Notifications are a Pro feature.</Note>

## Notification Types

### Goal Achievement

Triggered when your step count reaches your daily goal. You'll see a celebratory message with your achievement. Sent at most once per day.

### Milestone

Triggered when you hit **50% of your daily goal**. Shows your progress percentage and how many steps remain to reach your target. Sent at most once per day.

### Workout Completion

Sent after you finish a workout session. Includes your workout duration and distance covered. The message varies based on workout type (walking, running, or cycling).

### Apps Unlocked

If you use [App Lock](/docs/features/app-lock), you'll receive a notification when your step goal is reached and your locked apps are unblocked. Sent at most once per day.

## Enabling Notifications

1. Open **Settings** in Steps
2. Toggle **Notifications** on
3. Allow notifications when prompted by iOS

## Disabling Notifications

Toggle **Notifications** off in Steps Settings to stop all notifications. You can also manage Steps notifications in your iPhone's **Settings > Notifications > Steps**.
